Frequently Asked Questions

Salon Policies

  • What are your cancellation policies?
    We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations.
  • Do you accept walk-ins?
    While we welcome walk-ins, we recommend booking appointments in advance to ensure availability.
  • What forms of payment do you accept?
    We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments.

Services

  • What services do you offer?
    We offer a wide range of hair and beauty services, including haircuts, styling, coloring, makeup, manicures, pedicures, and more.
  • Can I book multiple services in one appointment?
    Yes, you can book multiple services in one appointment based on availability.
  • Do you offer consultations?
    Yes, we offer complimentary consultations to discuss your hair and beauty needs.

Salon Amenities

  • Is parking available?
    Yes, we have parking available for our clients.
  • Is the salon accessible for individuals with disabilities?
    Yes, our salon is fully accessible.
  • What amenities do you provide?
    We provide complimentary beverages, Wi-Fi, and a comfortable waiting area for our clients.
