Appointment Booking

Welcome to Southlink Salon's appointment booking system. We strive to make the booking process as seamless as possible for our clients.

Instructions for Booking Appointments:

  • Visit our website and navigate to the appointment booking page.
  • Select the service you'd like to book.
  • Choose your preferred date and time for the appointment.
  • Provide your contact information and any special requests or notes.
  • Confirm your appointment details and submit your booking.

Policies:

We value your time and ours. Please review our policies regarding cancellations, rescheduling, and payment methods:

  • Cancellations: We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations.
  • Rescheduling: If you need to reschedule your appointment, please contact us as soon as possible.
  • No-shows: Clients who do not show up for their appointments without prior notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • Payment Methods: We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments. Payment is due at the time of service.

Note: Our team will reach out to confirm your appointment shortly after receiving your booking request. Thank you for choosing Southlink Salon!
